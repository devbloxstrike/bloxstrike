## 🐛 Common Issues and Fixes

### ❌ GUI doesn't appear after execution
**Fix:** Make sure your executor is fully attached to Roblox *before* executing the script. Detach, reattach, then re-execute. Also confirm you are inside an active BloxStrike match — the GUI will not render in the lobby screen.

### ❌ Aimbot locks onto teammates
**Fix:** Open the Aimbot tab in the GUI and enable the **Team Check** toggle. This filters out players on your team from the targeting pool.

### ❌ Wall Bang module has no effect
**Fix:** Wall Bang only works on penetrable surface types (thin walls, fences, crates). Solid concrete and terrain objects are excluded by design. Confirm you're shooting at a supported surface type.

### ❌ ESP boxes are flickering or misaligned
**Fix:** This typically happens when Roblox's rendering resolution changes mid-session (e.g., switching from fullscreen to windowed). Toggle ESP off and back on to force a recalculation of screen-space coordinates.

### ❌ Script stops working after a Roblox update
**Fix:** Game updates invalidate certain API hooks the script relies on. Check this repository's Releases page for a patch. In the meantime, `bloodstrike script no key` community forks may offer temporary workarounds while the main update is in progress.

### ❌ Hitbox Expander causes rubber-banding
**Fix:** Reduce the hitbox scale from the default 5× down to 2–3×. Extremely high hitbox values (8–10×) can conflict with Roblox's server-side position validation and cause visible rubber-banding.

### ❌ No Recoil not working on specific weapons
**Fix:** Some weapons in BloxStrike use a non-standard recoil pattern. Open the **Misc** tab and enable **Force No Spread** alongside No Recoil — using both together covers all weapon classes in the current build.
